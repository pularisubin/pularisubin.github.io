About

I am an Assistant Professor in the Big Data and Information Retrieval School, Faculty of Computer Science, HSE University Moscow, where I have been since 2025. Previously, I was a CNRS postdoctoral researcher at LaBRI, Université de Bordeaux (2024–2025), hosted by Laurent Bienvenu. I received my Ph.D. and M.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Kanpur in 2024, advised by Satyadev Nandakumar and Sunil Simon. I obtained my B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from NIT Calicut in 2018.

My primary interests are algorithmic information theory, computational complexity, and computable analysis.
